Section 05

Layer Classification

Understanding Layer 1 and Layer 2 architecture

Layer 1 (L1)

The base blockchain protocol that processes and finalizes transactions on its own network. L1 chains provide security, consensus, and data availability at the foundational level.

Layer 2 (L2)

Secondary frameworks or protocols built on top of Layer 1 to improve scalability. L2 solutions batch transactions off-chain and settle periodically on the base layer.
